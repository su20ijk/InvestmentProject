Mutual Fund

A mutual fund is a type of financial tool that is made up of a pool of money collected from poeple and being used to invest in a combination of bonds and stocks.

Mutual funds are divesified as the money is used to invest in a combination of stocks and bonds.
Mutual fonds are simple. The professionals make the decision for you.
You can decide the cost. There is no limit about how much money you put into the pool.

Pros Cons
  • Professionals will make the decision for you
  • You can choose any amount of money
  • Mutual fund is diversified
  • Mutual funds have low growth
  • Mutual funds have little control by the investors.
